Features

Every Tool You Need to Stop Wasting Food

FreshSense combines real-time chemical detection, machine learning, and smart notifications into a single, elegant experience. Here's everything that comes in the box — and on your phone.

🔬

Real-Time VOC Gas Detection

Continuously monitors ethylene, ammonia, hydrogen sulfide, and CO₂ concentrations inside your fridge using an array of MOS gas sensors — detecting spoilage at the molecular level.

🤖

AI Freshness Classification

Our hybrid Random Forest + LSTM model classifies food into three clear states — Fresh, Caution, and Spoiled — so you always know where every item stands.

⏱️

Spoilage Countdown Timer

See exactly how many hours of freshness remain for each food item. No more guessing based on printed expiry dates — this is biochemistry-driven prediction.

🔔

Push Notifications

Receive smart alerts on your phone before food crosses from the Caution zone to Spoiled. Timely enough to act — cook it, freeze it, or share it.

🍳

AI Recipe Suggestions

When ingredients enter the Caution state, FreshSense generates personalized recipe suggestions using those exact items — turning potential waste into delicious meals.

📊

Weekly Waste Report

Track how much food you've saved, how much waste you've prevented, and your estimated dollar savings — all in a beautiful weekly summary delivered to the app.

🧊

Universal Fridge Compatibility

FreshSense is a retrofit module that sits on any standard refrigerator shelf. No appliance replacement, no drilling, no rewiring — works with what you already have.

📱

Cross-Platform Mobile App

Built with React Native for both iOS and Android, the FreshSense app delivers a unified experience — real-time dashboards, countdown timers, recipes, and notifications.
