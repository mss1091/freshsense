How It Works

From Gas Molecules to Actionable Insights

Three simple steps between your fridge shelf and real-time freshness intelligence on your phone.

Step 01
🧪

Sense

MOS gas sensors detect volatile organic compounds — ethylene, ammonia, H₂S — emitted by food as it decays.

Step 02
🧠

Analyze

Our cloud-based ML model classifies food freshness into Fresh, Caution, or Spoiled and predicts hours remaining.

Step 03
📱

Act

Get push notifications, spoilage countdowns, and AI-generated recipe suggestions before food goes to waste.
